How cool are generators?

Friday 13 Jan 2013

OMG!! Generators are like the freekin bog's dollocks!

Like, check it out:

>>> a = [1, 2, 3, 4, 5]
>>> b = (2*x for x in a)
>>> b.next()
2

Seriously, like, WOW!!!one!11!1!eleven!!!
